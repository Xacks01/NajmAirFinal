Our Story

A Legacy of Excellence in the Skies

Founded with a vision to transform air travel, Najm Air has grown from a boutique charter service to a global leader in private aviation and ACMI solutions. We believe that the journey is just as important as the destination.

Our commitment goes beyond flying; it's about crafting personalized experiences. Whether it's a critical business trip, a pilgrimage for Hajj & Umrah, or a leisure getaway, our fleet and crew are dedicated to your safety, comfort, and schedule.
