Frequently asked Questions

How do I book a private charter flight?

Booking is simple! You can request a quote directly through our website's "Book a Flight" button, or contact our 24/7 support team via phone or email. We'll tailor a travel solution to your specific needs.

What destinations does Najm Air fly to?

We operate globally, connecting you to over 200 destinations worldwide. Whether it's a remote island or a major business hub, if there's an airport, we can likely take you there.

Do you offer specialized packages for Hajj and Umrah?

Yes, we specialize in organizing group charter flights for Hajj and Umrah pilgrimages. We work closely with travel agencies to ensure a spiritual and comfortable journey for all pilgrims.

What is the baggage allowance for charter flights?

Charter flights typically offer more generous baggage allowances than commercial airlines. However, limits depend on the specific aircraft type. Our team will advise you on the capacity of your chosen jet.

Can I travel with my pet?

Absolutely! We are a pet-friendly airline. Your furry companions can travel in the cabin with you, ensuring they are safe and comfortable throughout the journey. Just let us know in advance!

What safety standards does Najm Air adhere to?

Safety is our non-negotiable priority. We adhere to strict international aviation safety standards (ICAO/IATA) and conduct rigorous maintenance and crew training to ensure your peace of mind.
